Cool Water is a legendary marine fragrance by Davidoff, introduced in 1988. It features a blend of mint, sea water, and rosemary as top notes, followed by lavender, jasmine, geranium, and neroli in the heart, and oakmoss, musk, and sandalwood in the base. This fragrance is known for its fresh, invigorating scent that evokes the essence of the ocean, making it a popular choice for both men and women. The iconic wave-shaped bottle, designed by Pierre Bourdon, symbolizes the fragrance's aquatic theme and has become synonymous with freshness and masculinity.
